Chapter 5: Microwave Safety

📌 WHY DO WE NEED MICROWAVE SAFETY?

Microwaves are powerful. We need to be careful so they don't hurt us.

Simple definition: Safety rules help us use microwaves without getting hurt.

Real-life example: When you fly in an airplane, they tell you to turn off your phone. Why? Because the phone's microwaves can mess with the airplane's instruments. That's microwave safety!

📌 WHY IS IT IMPORTANT?

Microwave safety is important because:

  • It keeps us safe from strong waves
  • It stops phones from breaking airplane systems
  • It protects hospital machines from interference

📌 SAFETY RULES — HOW DO WE STAY SAFE?

Let me explain the simple rules:

  • Rule 1: Turn off your phone in airplanes (it's the law!)
  • Rule 2: Don't stand too close to big microwave machines
  • Rule 3: Never open a microwave oven while it's running
  • Rule 4: Hospitals have special "no phone" zones for safety

🔁 LET'S REMEMBER:

  • Microwaves are powerful — be careful
  • Airplanes need phones OFF for safety
  • Hospitals protect machines from waves
  • Don't open microwave oven while on

  • Microwave Safety = Stay safe
  • Airplane mode = No signals
