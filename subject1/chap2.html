Chapter 2: Microwave Oven

📌 WHAT IS A MICROWAVE OVEN?

A microwave oven is a box that heats your food using microwaves.

Simple definition: It is a machine that uses invisible waves to make food hot.

Real-life example: When mom heats milk at home, she puts the cup in the microwave oven. After 1 minute, the milk is hot! The oven uses microwaves to do this.

📌 WHY IS IT IMPORTANT?

Microwave ovens are important because:

  • It heats food very fast — saves time
  • It is easy to use — just press a button
  • It uses microwaves to heat from inside out

📌 HOW DOES IT WORK?

Let me explain in simple steps:

  • Step 1: You put food inside the microwave oven
  • Step 2: The oven makes microwaves (invisible waves)
  • Step 3: The waves go into the food
  • Step 4: Water in the food vibrates and makes heat
  • Step 5: The food becomes hot and ready to eat!

🔁 LET'S REMEMBER:

  • Microwave oven heats food fast
  • It uses invisible microwave waves
  • Waves make water vibrate
  • Vibration creates heat

📝 SIMPLE NOTES:

  • Microwave Oven = Heats food fast
  • Uses: Invisible microwave waves
  • Waves → vibrate water → heat
  • Like a magic box for heating!
