Chapter 1: What is Electronics?

📌 WHAT IS ELECTRONICS?

Electronics is about using electricity to make things work.

Simple definition: It is the study of how to use electric power to run devices.

Real-life example: When you press the button on your TV remote, electronics inside it sends a signal to turn on the TV. When you switch on a fan, electronics makes it spin!

📌 WHY IS IT IMPORTANT?

Electronics is important because:

  • It makes your TV, fan, and bulb work
  • It is inside every device you use at home
  • It helps us control electricity safely

📌 HOW DOES IT WORK?

Let me explain in simple steps:

  • Step 1: Electricity flows from a battery or plug
  • Step 2: Wires carry the electricity like pipes carry water
  • Step 3: Electronic parts control where the electricity goes
  • Step 4: The device (like a fan) turns ON and works!

🔁 LET'S REMEMBER:

  • Electronics = Using electricity to make things work
  • Electricity flows like water in pipes
  • TV remote, fan, bulb all use electronics
  • Wires carry electricity to devices

📝 SIMPLE NOTES:

  • Electronics = Electricity control
  • TV remote = Electronics
  • Fan, bulb = Use electronics
  • Wires = Like water pipes
