Chapter 5: Power Supply

📌 WHAT IS A POWER SUPPLY?

A power supply gives electricity to a device. It changes the electricity from the wall to the right type for your device.

Simple definition: It is like a water pump that gives the right amount of water to your device.

Real-life example: Your phone charger is a power supply. It takes big electricity from the wall and changes it to small electricity that your phone can use safely!

📌 WHY IS IT IMPORTANT?

Power supply is important because:

  • It gives devices the power they need
  • It protects devices from too much electricity
  • Without it, devices would burn or not work

📌 HOW DOES IT WORK?

Let me explain in simple steps:

  • Step 1: Power supply takes electricity from the wall
  • Step 2: It changes big high electricity to small safe electricity
  • Step 3: It sends the right amount to your device
  • Step 4: Your device runs safely and happily!

🔁 LET'S REMEMBER:

  • Power Supply = Gives electricity
  • Changes wall power to device power
  • Phone charger = Power supply
  • Protects devices from damage

📝 SIMPLE NOTES:

  • Power Supply = Electricity giver
  • Wall → Charger → Phone
  • Big power → Small safe power
  • Like a water pump for power
