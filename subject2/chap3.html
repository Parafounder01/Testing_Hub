Chapter 3: Simple Circuits

📌 WHAT IS A SIMPLE CIRCUIT?

A simple circuit is a circle of electricity that makes a device work.

Simple definition: It is a loop that electricity travels around.

Real-life example: An LED light with a battery and a switch. When you turn the switch ON, electricity goes from battery → LED → back to battery. The LED lights up!

📌 WHY IS IT IMPORTANT?

Simple circuits are important because:

  • It is the start of all electronics
  • Every device has a circuit inside it
  • You can build one with just a battery and LED!

📌 HOW DOES IT WORK?

Let me explain in simple steps:

  • Step 1: Connect a battery to a switch
  • Step 2: Connect the switch to an LED light
  • Step 3: Connect the LED back to the battery
  • Step 4: Turn ON the switch — the circle is complete!
  • Step 5: Electricity flows and LED lights up!

🔁 LET'S REMEMBER:

  • Circuit = Circle of electricity
  • Battery → Switch → LED → Battery
  • Complete circle = Light turns ON
  • Break the circle = Light turns OFF

📝 SIMPLE NOTES:

  • Circuit = Loop
  • Battery gives power
  • LED lights up
  • Circle must be complete
