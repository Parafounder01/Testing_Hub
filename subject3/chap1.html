Chapter 1: What is Circuit Design?

📌 WHAT IS CIRCUIT DESIGN?

Circuit design is planning how to connect electronic parts to make a device work.

Simple definition: It is like drawing a map before building a house.

Real-life example: Before making a real circuit, you draw it on paper. You decide where the battery goes, where the LED goes, and how to connect them with wires. That drawing is circuit design!

📌 WHY IS IT IMPORTANT?

Circuit design is important because:

  • It helps you plan before building
  • It saves time — no mistakes later
  • It makes sure the circuit will work!

📌 HOW DOES IT WORK?

Let me explain in simple steps:

  • Step 1: Decide what you want the circuit to do (make LED blink?)
  • Step 2: Draw the parts you need (battery, LED, resistor)
  • Step 3: Draw lines to show how to connect them
  • Step 4: Check your drawing — will it work?

🔁 LET'S REMEMBER:

  • Circuit Design = Planning before building
  • Like drawing a map before a trip
  • You draw parts and connections
  • Check the drawing before building!
